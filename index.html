Welcome to the CSC380 Group D Track Builder
  • Editing your track -
  • To add a new track piece, click the grid location where you want it and select the type of piece from the context menu.
  • To edit existing pieces, click on them and select the desired action from the context menu.
  • To move a piece just click and drag the piece to where you want it!
  • Viewing your track -
  • You can either right click and drag or click the arrow buttons in the top right corner to move your track around.
  • To zoom in and out, scroll the mouse wheel or click the magnifying glasses on the right side of the screen.
  • To reset the screen, click the icon on the right, between the magnifying glasses
  • You can swap between the layers using the icon on the right
  • Track Generator -
  • Clicking the icon in the tool bar will bring up the Track Generator dialog. Here you can customize what pieces it will use to generate tracks. You can also set a limit to the number of consecutive straight and corner pieces it will use.
    • Once generation has started, the most interesting and distinct tracks will be displayed. You click on a track to view it in more detail, then if it suits you click "SELECT" to load that track in the design area.
  • After selecting a track the system will continue attempting to generating tracks, indicated by the green spinner on the icon.
  • If you click the icon again, it will return you to the Track Pool. From here you can either select another track or change the settings of Track Generator.
  • Note: Intersections, layer transitions and jumps exponentially increase the number of possible options for tracks and thus can exponentially increase the amount of time before valid tracks are generated.
  • Other features -
  • You can download your track with the icon to use later or share with others.
  • With the icon you can load saved tracks.
  • Use the icon to start over with a clean slate.
  • To remove any unconnected pieces click the icon.
  • To get a list of all the parts used in the current track, click the icon.
